# Background — Blueprint (single, shared)

The one reusable base layer. Generate this **once**, then composite a transparent ticket
on top in Canva. The centre is kept relatively uncluttered so a ticket sits cleanly over it.

**Output:** flat **square** 1024×1024, no rounded corners (iOS masks at display time),
full-bleed, no subject — background only.

## Prompt

```
A square image 1024×1024, flat square with no rounded corners, background only (no subject, no objects). Apple "blueprint" style: a bright blue gradient running from sky-blue in the top-left (#5FA8FF) through mid-blue (#2C7BEE) to a deep blue in the bottom-right (#1457C8). Overlay a faint white draftsman's grid — a fine 64px grid plus a stronger 256px grid, both at ~15–20% opacity. Add subtle white concentric guide circles centred on the canvas, and a faint horizontal and vertical centre line — all at low opacity, evoking an engineer's blueprint. Do not add any corner ticks or registration marks in the corners. Keep the central area relatively clean and uncluttered (room to place a subject on top later). Even, soft lighting, no vignette beyond a gentle deepening toward the bottom-right. Square image 1024×1024, full-bleed.
```
